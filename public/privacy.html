Privacy Notice

Bucket of Thoughts is a personal productivity workspace maintained by the Device Dev project team. We take privacy seriously and only collect the data needed to deliver the app.

What we collect

  • Your Google account profile (name, email, and profile photo) after you sign in. This is provided by Google and used solely to show your account and associate ideas with you.
  • Ideas, categories, and related metadata that you create inside the app. These are stored securely in our Firebase project so they stay in sync across your devices.
  • Limited local cache data so the app can work quickly when offline. This data stays on your device unless you clear your browser storage.

What we never collect

  • Your Google password or any login credential. Authentication runs entirely through Google.
  • Sensitive personal information beyond what you choose to write in your notes.
  • Analytics or advertising identifiers. We do not track you across other sites.

How we use your data

Your data is used only to deliver core features: capture, review, and organize ideas. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties outside of Firebase services required to operate the product.

Your choices

  • You can delete your notes at any time inside the app.
  • If you sign out, cached data remains on your device until you clear it from your browser (Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data).
  • To request data deletion from our Firebase project, contact us using the email below and include your Google account address.
